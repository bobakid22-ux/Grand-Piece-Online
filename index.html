Welcome to Grand Piece Online

Explore the world of Grand Piece Online, a Roblox adventure where you can collect Devil Fruits, master weapons, and conquer seas!

Devil Fruits

Gomu Gomu no Mi - Rubber powers: stretch your body.
Mera Mera no Mi - Fire powers: control flames.
Hie Hie no Mi - Ice powers: freeze enemies.
Pika Pika no Mi - Light powers: super speed.
Goro Goro no Mi - Lightning powers: shock attacks.

Weapons

Swords - Perfect for close combat and PvP battles.
Guns - Ranged attacks for hitting enemies from afar.
Axes - Heavy damage but slower attacks.
Staffs - Medium range, versatile combat.
Bows - Long-range attacks with precision.
